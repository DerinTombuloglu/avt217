What Are We About?

Art news is a news publisher started by the Thompson brothers, Bobby Thompson, and Austin Thompson, on august 23, 2005. The whole idea of this publishing paper was to give artists a voice so that their work can be recognized and get the credit for their ideas and excel their careers to new heights.We here at art news are working hard to ensure that every artist that we come across gets a voice and a platform to show their art. Here at Art News we are committed to encouraging a safe and healthy environment for our employees where they can feel accepted for who they are and express themselves through their own work. Here at Art News we encourage diversity and difference of opinion as we wan to foster a community of acceptance, inclusivity, and creativity.
