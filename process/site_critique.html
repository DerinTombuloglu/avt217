National Art Gallery

https://www.nga.gov/

Navigation.

I felt that the site was easy to navigate and had clear directions. It was easy to find the page where I wanted to go.

Usability

The usability is fine and I had no problems with the website's performance. The site even has audio transcripts for the visually impared.

Typography and hierarchy

The font used is very stylized and very clean which fits appropriately for this site and structure for this site is well composed.

Overall style/look and feel

The site is accessible, easy to navigate,has great text structure, and a good font for the style. There's not really anything bad about this site in my opinion.
